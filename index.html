Alibid Debate and Speech Society International

We promote the art of debate and speeches internationally.

Alibid Society is a non-profit organization run by high school students, focused on the promotion of debate and public speaking amongst youth grade one to twelve. Through our interactive programs, members are able to gain confidence and knowledge. In addition, we offer incredible internships and volunteering opportunities.

Leadership

We offer monthly internship opportunities such as tournament directors and organizers, periodic programs such as the summer speech program in which we recruit teachers and project managers, and much more
