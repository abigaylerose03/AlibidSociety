Get Started


Check out our multitudes of programs designed with students in mind. Not only can you benefit knowledge wise, you can also obtain CAS and volunteer hours in some of our sessions.

Regular Program

Designed for students grade 6-12, this program promotes the art of debate and speeches through interactive workshops, lessons, and debate tournaments. It is completely free and no experience is required.

Summer Program

Designed for students grade 1-6, this speech summer camp is carefully designed to promote confidence. The entire course is 6 weeks long and packed with valuable information. It costs $30-45. No experience is required.
