Regular Programme, Gr 6-12

Members can join our regular debate programme online. With weekly debate and speech lessons with keynote speakers from university debating society across North America and Europe, and other speech organizations like the Toastmasters, members learn and gain confidence effortlessly through interactive lessons and tournaments. No previous experience required. Internship and volunteering opportunities are also available within our program. For example, we regularly roll out monthly debate tournaments, and we have a selection of monthly tournament directors who will be doing outreach, organizing, and internal communicating. In addition, we are launching a brand new Ambassador program in the near future. These opportunities are only open to registered members, so if you want to gain leadership experiences, sign up now!

Summer Speech Camp, Gr 1-6

Targeted toward younger audiences, students can join a class of thirty to forty-five minutes every week to an upward of six weeks for a series of interactive speech lessons and practice opportunities. This course is carefully crafted and is designed to boost confidence amongst children. All money gained from camps will be used to pay the expenses at Alibid Society. No previous experiences required. Our teaching team comprises experienced students with extensive backgrounds in public speaking and the mentoring of young students. Some of our teaching team have qualified and competed in provincial ADSA debate tournaments, winning awards such as top novice team in Alberta. Others have competed in provincial and national speech competitions and won first places. One from our team has spoken at Rideau Hall in Ottawa for the Imagine a Canada contest in front of MPs, Lieutenant Governors, and the Governor-General.
